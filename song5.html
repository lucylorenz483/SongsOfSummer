5. Psilocybae (Millenial Love)

Performer

Childish Gambino

Review

Donald Glover or also known as Childish Gambino never seems to fail with creating amazing, non before heard music that always has an electic beat. This summer of 2024 Childish Gambino came out with a new ablum called Atavista with many top hits. But by far the most different but amazing work he did was on the song Psilocybae. His alterations and vocals within this song are out of this world, but not just his the features within this song as well. In the song 21 Savage is featured with his own verse in the song that shifts the song into a complete different beat. This could have been disartusly but with the help of the other features Ink & Kadhj Bonet they create a wonderful tanstion that keeps this song alive.

Rating

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4/5 stars)
