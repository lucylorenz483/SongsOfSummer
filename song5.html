5. Psilocybae (Millenial Love)

Performer

Childish Gambino

Review

Donald Glover, also known as Childish Gambino, never seems to fail at creating amazing, never-before-heard music that always has an eclectic beat. In the summer of 2024, Childish Gambino released a new album called Atavista, featuring many top hits. By far, the most unique and amazing work on the album is the song Psilocybae. His vocal alterations in this song are out of this world, and the features add even more depth. The song includes a verse by 21 Savage that shifts the beat completely, which could have been disastrous, but with the help of the other features, Ink & Kadhja Bonet, they create a wonderful transition that keeps the song alive.

Rating

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4/5 stars)
