8. OMG

Performer

Suki Waterhouse

Review

Suki Waterhouse another british indie artist. Her spunky music has caught quite a lot of peoples attention in recent years. Earlier this summer she came out with a single called OMG. It is about finding love and being consumed by it. That when you fall in love you lose a part of who you were in the past before you were in love. It is a relatable song and as sad as the topic is Suki Waterhouse creates a zippy pop indie song that you could sing along to in the shower.

Rating

⭐⭐⭐☆☆ (3/5 stars)
