2. Juna

Performer

Clairo

Review

Clairo is new ablum called Charm came out in the summer 2024. One of the best ablums of the summer, but if I had to choose one of the songs to be on this list it has to be Juna. Juna is a upbeat but mellow song that just brings the sun out on any summer day.

Rating

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4/5 stars)
