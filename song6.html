6. Tough

Performer

Lana Del Ray Featuring Quavo

Review

Lana Del Rey, known for her mesmerizing storytelling and haunting vocals, teamed up with rapper Quavo to create one of the standout tracks of the summer. The song starts off with a slow, sultry beat that sets the mood, enhanced by Lana's captivating voice. As the track progresses, Quavo enters with his verses, bringing a fresh, laid-back energy that perfectly complements Lana's style. His rhythmic flow and Lana's ethereal background vocals blend seamlessly, creating a unique fusion of their distinct sounds. The result is a chill, upbeat song that captures the essence of summer, making it a must-have on any playlist. This unexpected collaboration showcases both artists' versatility and ability to push musical boundaries, proving once again why they are at the top of their game.

Rating

⭐⭐⭐☆☆ (3/5 stars)
